This page allows to view and manage team members and their roles. More on that [here](https://docs.vast.ai/teams-roles). To create a team, follow this [guide](https://docs.vast.ai/teams-quickstart).

# Page Walkthrough

Here's an example of what a Members page would look like in the console.

![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/9WtD9F5n1L2IYJxS0qXDd/pkpelXLvq0cdGr1JQ458r_image.png)

You can change a role by clicking on directional arrow and selecting a new role.



![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/9WtD9F5n1L2IYJxS0qXDd/fv5wVcGdfHNUtv_jHY8iX_image.png "Roles")

You can also remove a teammate by clicking on 'Delete', which will trigger a pop-up.



![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/9WtD9F5n1L2IYJxS0qXDd/lMoZrFkS6RbFK66a9fMhr_image.png "Remove Member")

As you can see from the images, this Members console page easily allows you to view and manage the current state of your team.
